Feels like warm autumn...

Waking up in the morning is so much more difficult when its raining and grey outside... Well, some people were up from 5am, just to get their new iPhones, so I don't think it really matters whats going on outside, when we are busy with our small human tasks... But my post is not about that, I am not going to start to look deeper in the human philosophy and consumer behavior, its still a bit too early for that... :)

I just offer you this few ideas of the looks for this rainy and grey weather (not common for Marbella, but might be useful for those who live 320 with what we experience here right now...)


Mango jacket, pants and blazer, with Hope boots and bag, Mango bracelet, Uterque glasses. My favorite color combination: grey-beige-brown.


Mango blouse, H&M skirt, Mango trench coat, Uterque bag, Christian Louboutin ankle boots Col Zippe, Bimba & Lola collar and ring.

And something for the night.. Yes, its Friday today, isn't it??...

Karen Millen dress, Miu Miu peep-toes, Mango jacket, Swarowski earrings and bracelet, ToyWatch Metallic Stones watch, Uterque clutch

Have fun today! We deserved it! :)

About Me

Nastasia Voronecka
My native language is Russian and I am also fluent in English, Spanish and Latvian languages. I have successfully graduated secondary school in Latvia and then continued my education in Spain, University of Valencia, Faculty of Tourism and Hospitality Management. After two courses in University of Valencia, I continued my studies in Business Administration High School “Turiba”, Faculty of International Tourism in Latvia. Four years ago I have completed my studies in International Hospitality and Tourism Management Programme in Brussels Business Institute in Brussels, Belgium.

During my university years I have successfully performed not only as a student, but I also have gained the working experience as an interpreter, while working with foreign journalists and professors from United States, Spain and England and I also took part in a process of an editorial work in the “International Entrepreneurship and Management Journal” (IEMJ) and in a special issue of this journal “Entrepreneurship and Learning Education”. 

After years of experience in the field of the real estate in Marbella, Spain, in 2015 I have founded a real estate company "LION SYNERGY GROUP" and managing it until now.
